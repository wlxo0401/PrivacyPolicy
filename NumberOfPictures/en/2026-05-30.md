## 2. Categories of Personal Data Processed

The App does not require an account, and we do not directly ask you to provide personal information. There is data you create/store on your device, and data collected automatically by third-party SDKs.

### 2-1. Data you create/store on your device (on-device only)

- **Photos** — photos you select are processed **only on your device** to perform the numbering feature and are **never uploaded to any server we control**. Saving the result to your camera roll (Photos app) is your choice.
- **Work history** — a record of your numbering actions: creation date, image name (reference), input mode. Stored **only in on-device SwiftData** and never transmitted.

### 2-2. Data collected automatically (by third-party SDKs / system)

This data is **not collected or stored directly by us; it is collected and processed automatically by the third-party SDKs integrated into the App**, each for its own purpose. We do not store this data in a directly identifiable form and only see aggregated/anonymized results.

| Category | Examples | Collected by |
|---|---|---|
| Device identifiers | IDFV | Firebase Analytics, Firebase Crashlytics, Google AdMob |
| Advertising identifier | IDFA (only when you consent via App Tracking Transparency) | Google AdMob |
| Device & environment | OS version, device model, app version, locale | Firebase Analytics, Firebase Crashlytics, Google AdMob |
| Approximate location | Country/region derived from IP address | Firebase Analytics, Google AdMob |
| Usage data | In-app events, screens viewed, feature interactions | Firebase Analytics |
| Diagnostic / crash data | Crash logs, performance metrics | Firebase Crashlytics |
| Advertising data | Ad impressions, clicks | Google AdMob |
| Advertising attribution | App install/conversion attribution | Apple SKAdNetwork |

## 8. California Residents (CCPA / CPRA)

If you are a California resident, in addition to the rights above you have the right to:
- **Know** what personal information is collected and how it is used
- **Delete** personal information collected from you
- **Correct** inaccurate personal information
- **Opt out of "sharing"** of personal information for cross-context behavioral advertising (under CPRA, advertising identifiers passed to AdMob may qualify as "sharing")
- **Limit the use of sensitive personal information** (we do not collect sensitive PI as defined by CPRA)
- **Non-discrimination** for exercising your rights

We do **not "sell"** personal information for monetary consideration. To opt out of tracking-based advertising, decline the App Tracking Transparency prompt or disable tracking in iOS Settings.

To submit a CCPA/CPRA request, email **wlxo0401@gmail.com**. We will verify your identity using reasonable methods before fulfilling the request.

## 9. Withdrawing Tracking / Consent

- iOS Settings → Privacy & Security → Tracking → Number on Photos (toggle off)
- In-app Google UMP consent form — when prompted, you can change your advertising/tracking consent choices
- iOS Settings → Privacy & Security → Apple Advertising → reset advertising identifier
- iOS Settings → Privacy & Security → Photos → Number on Photos (change access)
- Uninstalling the App removes all on-device local data (work history, settings)
